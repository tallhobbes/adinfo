Research Overview


Analyzing learning environments through the motivation framework of Self-Determination Theory has led to some interesting findings - most recently including evidence that using discussion, rather than lecture, in class can meaningfully improve students' personal investment.

I'm still working to find the best way to present these materials online, but in the meantime you can click this tile to see all my published research hosted on ResearchGate.
